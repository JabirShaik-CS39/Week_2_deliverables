---------- Embeddings ----------
--> Embeddings in RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
--> Embeddings are one of the most important concepts in RAG systems.
--> They help computers understand the meaning of text instead of just matching keywords.

1. Embeddings

--> An embedding is a numerical vector representation of text.
--> It converts words, sentences, or documents into lists of numbers that capture semantic meaning.

Example:

"I love AI"

might become:

[0.21, -0.44, 0.91, 0.11, ...]

Another sentence with similar meaning:

"I enjoy artificial intelligence"

will produce a vector close to the first one.

2. Embeddings are Important in RAG

# RAG systems work like this:

User Query
     ↓
Convert Query → Embedding
     ↓
Search Similar Document Embeddings
     ↓
Retrieve Relevant Chunks
     ↓
Send Context to LLM
     ↓
Generate Answer

 Without embeddings:

--> Search becomes keyword-based
--> Results are less intelligent

With embeddings:

--> Semantic search becomes possible
--> Meaning is understood
--> Similar concepts are matched

3. Real-Life Example

Suppose your PDF contains:

"Python is widely used in machine learning."

User asks:
"Where is Python used in AI?"

Keyword search may fail because:
"machine learning" ≠ "AI"

Embedding search succeeds because:
embeddings understand semantic similarity

4. How Embeddings Work Internally
Step 1 — Tokenization

Text is split into tokens.

Example:
"Artificial Intelligence"

becomes:
["Artificial", "Intelligence"]

Step 2 — Neural Network Processing
A transformer model processes tokens and learns:

--> context
--> grammar
--> relationships
--> semantics

Step 3 — Vector Generation
The model outputs a high-dimensional vector.

Example:
[0.12, -0.91, 0.33, 0.77, ...]

Typical dimensions:

384
768
1024
1536

depending on the model.

5. Similarity Search

RAG compares vectors using mathematical distance.

Most common:

Cosine Similarity

Measures angle between vectors.

Formula
similarity = A · B / (|A| |B|)

Values:

Score	Meaning
1.0	Very similar
0.8	Similar
0.5	Somewhat related
0.0	Unrelated
